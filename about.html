🌟 About InAmigos Foundation 🌟

📅 Founded: 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO)

🏛️ Type: Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by Central Government

📜 Certifications: 80G & 12A, CSR‑1, ISO 9001:2015 | Recognized by NGO Darpan (NITI Aayog)

✨ Vision: Empowering Lives, Spreading Compassion

🎯 Mission: To foster community development and social empowerment through education 📚, healthcare 🏥, environment protection 🌱, women empowerment 👩‍🦱, animal welfare 🐾, and youth skill-building 💡.

🤝 Our specialties include fundraising, volunteering, crowd funding, virtual volunteering, and community influencing. With 10,000+ members and strong CSR partnerships, we believe in collective action to create lasting impact.

🌍 Funds raised are pooled together and allocated towards food 🍲, water 💧, education 📖, and social inclusion initiatives across India.

🚀 We actively run six flagship projects: Seva (food & essentials), Bachpanshala (education for children), Udaan (women empowerment), Prakriti (environment protection), Jeev (animal welfare), and Vikas (youth skill-building).

📊 Impact: 50,000+ meals served | 20,000+ trees planted | 30,000+ interns trained | 50+ stray animals fed daily

📱 Social Media: Active on LinkedIn (95k+ followers), Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube with campaigns like Meals of Hope, She Can Fly, and Plant for a Better Tomorrow.
