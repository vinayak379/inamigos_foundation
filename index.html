About InAmigos Foundation

Founded in 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to fostering community development and empowerment. Certified under 80G & 12A, CSR-1, and ISO 9001:2015, we strive to bridge gaps in education, healthcare, and opportunities for underprivileged communities.

🌟 Our Flagship Projects 🌟

🍲 Seva

Providing meals, clothes, and essentials to underprivileged communities.

📚 Bachpanshala

Free learning hubs for children, bridging educational gaps.

👩‍🦱 Udaan

Empowering women through self-defense, hygiene drives, and training.
